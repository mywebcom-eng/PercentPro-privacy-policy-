Privacy Policy for PercentPro

Effective Date: June 2026

PercentPro respects your privacy. This application is designed to operate entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Collect

PercentPro does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

Local Storage

PercentPro may store your calculation history locally on your device to improve your experience. This information remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any external server or third party.

Internet Access

PercentPro does not require an internet connection for its core functionality and does not transmit user data over the internet.

PercentPro does not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or external data collection services.

Data Security

Because all calculation data remains stored locally on your device, you maintain full control over your information. No user data is collected or stored by the developer.

Children's Privacy

PercentPro is suitable for users of all ages. The application does not knowingly collect, use, or share personal information from children or any other users.
